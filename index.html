The Legacy of Lola's Grill

At Lola Grill & Restaurant, every flame-grilled dish is a tribute to the authentic Filipino flavors passed down through generations. Founded and owned by visionary women from Bacolod, we pride ourselves on using traditional "Inasal" techniques and secret family marinades.

Our kitchen isn't just about food; it's about the warmth of a Filipino home. We source our ingredients locally to support Bacolod's farmers, ensuring that every bite of our Liempo or SISIG is as fresh as it is flavorful.
